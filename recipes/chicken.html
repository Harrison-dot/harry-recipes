Chicken Recipe

Back to main page

Description

To make a quick and quirky pie topping speedy scones are the best and taste utterly divine

Ingredients:

  • 200 g frozen mixed vegetables
  • 300 g tin cream of tomato soup
  • 175 g self-raising flour, plus extra to dust
  • 200 g cooked, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces

Steps to cook:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (180°C fan) mark 6. In a medium bowl, stir together the cooked chicken, frozen vegetables, soup and some seasoning. Pour the mixture into a rough 1 litre shallow ovenproof dish and set aside.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder and a large pinch of salt into a large bowl. Stir in most of the cheese. Beat the milk, egg and oil together in a separate bowl.
  3. Cook in the oven for 20min or until scones are risen and golden, and the filling is bubbling and piping hot. Serve immediately.
