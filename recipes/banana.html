Bananas recipe

Back to main page

Description

The first way to cook bananas involves frying the bananas in ghee with cardamom, ginger powder, and optional cinnamon.
This way of cooking the banana invigorates the senses in anticipation.
It starts with the buttery aroma of the ghee sweetened with spices.
Then the tropical mix wafts in from the banana, followed by the gentle flip to reveal a crispy, golden-brown crust.

Ingredients

  • Piece organic banana
  • Half cup water
  • Quarter teaspoon cinnamon stick
  • Half teaspoon cardamom powder

Steps to cook:

  1. Slice the tips off each end of the banana, then cut the banana into 1.5-inch lengths.
  2. Add water, ghee, cinnamon, cardamom, and salt to a small saucepan. Add the prepared banana. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Serve warm with the skins still on. Remove the skins before enjoying the banana.
